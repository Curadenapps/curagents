# BOB Measurement → iTOP Intervention Thresholds

Source: BOB iTOP Positioning Brief + PBE PRD

> This file maps BOB App measurement outputs to iTOP intervention tiers.
> Used by the session-template procedure to pre-populate intervention focus areas.

---

## BOB Index (Bleeding on Brushing %)

Measured post-brushing (T1 only — never pre-brush).

| BOB% | Tier | Colour code | iTOP Intervention Focus |
|------|------|-------------|------------------------|
| < 10% | Maintenance | 🟢 Green | Reinforce current technique, extend recall, positive reinforcement |
| 10–25% | Improvement | 🟡 Amber | Correct sulcular technique, review interdental care frequency |
| > 25% | Intensive | 🔴 Red | Full iTOP programme re-introduction, increase session frequency, consider adjuncts |

---

## PCR — Plaque Control Record (O'Leary %)

Measured at T0 (pre-brush) and T1 (post-brush).

| PCR% (T0) | Tier | iTOP Intervention Focus |
|-----------|------|------------------------|
| < 10% | Maintenance | Technique is effective — sustain |
| 10–30% | Improvement | Focus on problem surfaces (proximal, lingual), disclosing agent use |
| > 30% | Intensive | Supervised brushing session, step-by-step re-training, disclosing at every visit |

**Plaque Reduction (T0 → T1):**
A reduction of > 50% in a single session indicates good technique potential —
focus on consistency. < 30% reduction suggests technique correction is the priority.

---

## MGI — Modified Gingival Index (Erythema 0–3)

Longitudinal only — tracked across visits, not within a session.

| Mean MGI | Clinical indication | iTOP Implication |
|----------|--------------------|--------------------|
| 0 | Healthy tissue | No gingival inflammation — maintain |
| 1 | Mild redness | Monitor — reinforce technique |
| 2 | Moderate inflammation | Technique correction + recall shortening |
| 3 | Severe inflammation | [CLINICIAN REVIEW REQUIRED] — may indicate systemic factors beyond iTOP scope |

---

## Combined Assessment — Session Routing

| BOB tier | PCR tier | Recommended session type |
|----------|----------|--------------------------|
| Maintenance | Maintenance | Re-check (standard recall) |
| Maintenance | Improvement | Follow-up — plaque focus |
| Improvement | Any | Follow-up — technique correction |
| Intensive | Any | Initial-equivalent — full iTOP re-introduction |

---

## Key Positioning Rules

- **BOB is the primary motivational metric** — it is the most patient-visible,
  immediate feedback loop (blood = behaviour)
- **PCR shows technique quality** — it closes the loop between what the patient
  does and what the result is
- **MGI is longitudinal context** — it tells the story of tissue response over time
- **Never aggregate P, B, E into a single score** — display separately per
  BOB iTOP Positioning Brief guidelines
